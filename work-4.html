Travel Beyond
landing page for a company that organizes trips to mars

ABOUT
This project was created to get advance reservations to the red planet. Currently you can't go yet, but Elon Musk and our dreams are working to get it. That is why we have created this platform to ensure our seat on the spaceship.

MAIN SOLUTION
We first look at the huge galaxy with our planet Mars approaching. There we realize what is to come. The use of black and white as a representation of infinity and technology, past and future, immensity and light.
