Filmood
Your author cinema app adapted to you

ABOUT
Filmood is an app created to help all those people who are passionate about the most artistic and special cinema. It helps them find those hidden movies on the market, adapting to their current mood, in order to connect more deeply with the viewer.

THE PROBLEM
We currently have many digital streaming platforms for series, movies, etc. We see big Hollywood productions, mass productions, and sometimes we don't find what really makes us who we are. We lack identity and reality, and connect emotionally to generate empathy and proximity with what we are seeing.

THE SOLUTION
The goal is to create an app that connects with your emotions and is visually enriching. A place where movies adapt to your mood to make you feel alive. A place where cinema goes beyond Hollywood. An author cinema app adapted to you.

ROLE
Benchmark - User Persona - UX / UI - Content - Prototyping
