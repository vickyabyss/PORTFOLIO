La tierra del olvido
Cultural activities that brings life in little towns.

"La tierra del olvido" is a cooperative designed to bring life in towns of emptied Spain. Events, outings, activities, culture, brotherhood, nature... Values that strengthen our lives in historic areas so that they never die.

This project is based on a personal theme, the emptied Spain. Using methodologies such as Desk Research, benchmark etc. To find out what would be a good option to create new projects that promote tourism to those areas. Once the idea, texts and copys were defined, it was prototyped in Figma.
To bring this project to reality, it was developed in Html5, CSS and Js.
