Cosmic Chaos Fest
App dedicated to the extreme and underground music fest based in Barcelona

An app dedicated to a music festival. Its function is to collect all the information of the bands, create a calendar and lists of favorites, as well as offer all the necessary information of the festival.

In this project, the Design Thinking methodology was used to create a user experience adapted to its archetypes. The previous information architecture, wireframes, wireflows, final prototype and testing were generated to create a scalable and iterable project.
